About Me

I am a third-year B.Tech student in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Christ College of Engineering, Kerala. I am driven by a curiosity to understand and improve systems, with interests in embedded systems, electronic design, and control engineering.

I value practical learning and have 1.5+ years of experience in 3D printing and rapid prototyping using FDM, enabling me to turn ideas into functional designs. My work with microcontrollers and real-time systems has strengthened my ability to build intelligent, interactive solutions.

I am also actively involved with STRIDE, contributing to socially impactful engineering projects. This experience has shaped my perspective on inclusive, user-focused design.

I aim to deepen my technical expertise while developing solutions that are innovative, accessible, and meaningful.
